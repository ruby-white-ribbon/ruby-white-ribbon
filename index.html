Ruby White Ribbon

By wearing a white ribbon to ruby community events, I pledge:

  1. 1 to speak out against all forms of violence, harassment and discrimination
  2. 2 to learn about and follow the code of conduct
  3. 3 to speak out when the code of conduct is not honored

What is Ruby White Ribbon?

Ruby White Ribbon is a way to express allegiance in opposition to violence, harassment and discriminating behavior in our Ruby community.

The white ribbon has been used in recent times as the symbol of violence against women, and related causes.

This page is not related to, or affiliated with any pre-existing campaign or organization. Although we share values with the campaigns listed below.
